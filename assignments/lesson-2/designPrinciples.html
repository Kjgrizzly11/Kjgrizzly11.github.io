Fitt's Law

ebay

ebay.com

While hard to see on a desktop browser. On my phone I am able to press the search bar, profile key, and shopping cart all with my right hand. (I am right handed so that's what hand I hold my phone with, also yes I bought a copy of Smash recently) Everything I could want from this site is accessible the moment I turn it on without having to adjust my thumb.

White Space and Clean Design

Google

google.com

Google's page is basically the master of white space. Everything is white except for where you are supposed to be focused. Making it almost painful to focus on anywhere else except for the logo and search bar.

Hick's Law

Amazon

amazon.com

Amazon's website exemplifies Hick's law by orgazning the search options by how customers interact with their site. While the ability is there to search by departments or to look at "Best Sellers" the obvious focus is the search bar.
