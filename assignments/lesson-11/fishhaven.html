About Fish Haven

The first settlement at Fish Haven was made in 1864. A post office called Fish Haven was established in 1867, and remained in operation until 1962. The community was so named because nearby Bear Lake is a favorite fishing spot. Originally named Rush Creek, it is an unincorporated community along the shores of Bear Lake in Bear Lake County, Idaho, United States. It is 4 km north of the Utah border.
